  • About Me






Originally from Colorado, I found interest in the digital arts quickly. My parents gifted me a Flip Video Camera on my 10th birthday, and since then my art production has only increased.

Through high school I took many film classes, but once I graduated and chose to continue my education at the University of Montana, I found that the Media Arts degree they offer fit me better than anything had before, allowing me to express myself without limitations, while also soon gaining a BA in Media Arts in December 2022.

I've had lots of fun combining digital art and physical, like through screenprinting, and being able to implement my designs I make on my computer into editioned prints.
