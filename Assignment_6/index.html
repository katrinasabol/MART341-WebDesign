French Dip

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp Italian herb mix
  • French baguette
  • Mayonaise
  • 1/2 lb Roast beef
  • Provolone cheese
  • Garlic salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350oF.
  2. Mix olive oil and Italian herb mix together.
  3. Cut baguette to size, spread olive oil onto both sides.
  4. Put bread in oven for 5 minutes.
  5. After taking bread out, spread a thin layer of mayonaise on both sides of the bread.
  6. Put roast beef and provolone on bread. Sprinkle with garlic salt.
  7. Put back in oven for 5 more minutes.
  8. Serve and enjoy with an optional au jus!
