What is the relationship between an IP address and the DNS?
  We access domains on web browsers, which interact through IP addresses. IP addresses are translated by DNS so browsers can load the correct information.

What is a cookie?
  A cookie is a small text file that holds pieces of data that keep track of a user, like their login information, preferences, or where they left off on a page.

What are the three essential parts of a URL?
  Protocol, domain, and path
