From Wikipedia:

Christopher Smith Jr., better known by his stage name Smino, is an
American rapper and singer from St. Louis, Missouri. Smino is
the founder of the musical collective Zero Fatigue with Bari, Monte Booker,
Jay2, and Ravyn Lenae. He is a member of the hip-hop supergroup Zoink Gang, with JID, Buddy and Guapdad 4000.
